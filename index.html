✨ About Me ✨

Hi! I'm Aaralyn, a passionate individual from the beautiful state of Kerala. I'm someone who believes in living life to the fullest through diverse interests and hobbies.

Whether it's the rhythm of K-pop (I'm a huge fan of BABYMONSTER, Stray Kids, ILLIT, KATSEYE & ENHYPEN!), the thrill of badminton, the tranquility of swimming, the magic of horse riding, or the creativity of crafting, I find joy in everything I do. I also love reading, writing stories, and playing table tennis!

My favorite animals are horses, elephants, deer, and sloths. When I'm not being active, you'll find me playing games like Cookie Run Kingdom or Tomodachi: Living the Dream. My philosophy is simple: embrace every moment and make the most of every skill I develop!
